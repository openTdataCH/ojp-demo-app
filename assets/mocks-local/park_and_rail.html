P+Rail – your parking space at the railway station.

Do you live somewhere without a station or with poor public transport links? Do you travel very early or very late? Or do you want to get to your appointment without wasting time looking for a parking space? Then make use of P+Rail.

Simply park your car at one of the 550 P+Rail stations and change there easily to the train. Not only is this practical and convenient, it’s eco-friendly and affordable too.

There is at least one parking space available at every P+Rail facility for passengers with reduced mobility.

The easiest way to book hourly and full-day parking is via the P+Rail app.

Hourly and full-day parking.

At P+Rail facilities, you can pay per hour or per day. Day tickets are available for a maximum of 7 days.

The price for a day ticket is between CHF 3 and CHF 20, depending on location.

P+Rail at your station

Hourly ticket:

  • P+Rail app
  • parking meter

Day ticket:

  • P+Rail app
  • parking meter
  • online
  • from the SBB Contact Center on 0848 44 63 88 (CHF 0.08/min.)
  • from the ticket machine
  • at the ticket counter

  • Tickets purchased from the on-site parking meter must be displayed visibly in the car. At most locations, parking tickets do not need to be displayed in the car. Please note the information provided on the on-site parking meter.
  • Tickets purchased via the P+Rail app, from ticket machines, on sbb.ch or at the counter are checked via the number plate and do not have to be displayed in the car.
  • Please note that you do not have a right to a parking space even if you buy a ticket in advance (no refunds). We therefore recommend that you park your car first and then buy a parking ticket wherever possible.
  • The conditions detailed above apply to P+Rail facilities owned by SBB. Different options may apply for the purchase and receipt of parking tickets at municipal or private car parks located at or near stations.
  • For detailed conditions, please see the current P+Rail GTCs.
P+Rail GTC

Monthly and Annual Passes.

P+Rail Monthly or Annual Passes are the best choice for all commuters. P+Rail Passes are only available in combination with a public transport travelcard (GA, Regional or Point-to-Point Travelcard).

  • You can purchase Monthly Passes for CHF 30 up to CHF 160, depending on location. 
  • Annual Passes cost between CHF 300 and CHF 1600.

  • To buy a P+Rail Monthly or Annual Pass, you will need a valid public transport travelcard (GA, Point-to-Point or Regional Travelcard). 
  • You can buy your P+Rail Pass online at sbb.ch/en/parking. You can do this with a public transport travelcard referenced on the SwissPass.  
  • If your public transport travelcard is not accepted as a basis for buying a P+Rail Pass when using the SBB Webshop, please contact a staffed point of sale or the SBB Contact Center. 

  • Please note that a P+Rail Pass does not give you the right to a free parking space (no refunds).
  • You can enter two different registration numbers on your P+Rail Pass – so for example, one for your second car. You cannot change the car number(s) while the permit is valid. If you need to change your number, we offer an exchange (pro rata refund and new purchase).
  • You can buy P+Rail Passes up to two months before the first day of validity. This is of particular benefit where P+Rail facilities are in great demand.
  • To avoid overoccupancy, the number of P+Rail Passes is restricted where the facilities are in great demand. Please renew or purchase your P+Rail Pass early on sbb.ch/en/parking or at the counter. The limit also applies to the renewal of existing P+Rail Passes. Holders of P+Rail Passes have no right to renewal.
  • Different options may apply for the purchase and receipt of parking tickets at municipal or private car parks located at or near stations. 
  • For detailed conditions, please see the current P+Rail GTCs.
P+Rail GTC

P+Rail app.

It couldn’t be easier: in the P+Rail app, select your P+Rail facility and pay for as many hours or days as you want very easily from home, on the move or after parking your vehicle on site. Then you have the parking meter with you at all times on your smartphone. 

Simple – personal – cash – and contactless.

The P+Rail app is available for iOSLink opens in new window. and AndroidLink opens in new window..

Get a flexible parking ticket everywhere.

Buy your parking ticket simply, quickly and conveniently using your smartphone, with no queuing or searching for coins.

Leave your car for longer than planned.

In the P+Rail app, you can buy parking tickets with a validity of a maximum of seven days. If you wish to park for longer, you can extend the validity period conveniently from anywhere in our world.

One login for everything. 

With your SwissPass login, you can purchase both your public transport ticket and your P+Rail ticket. Still don’t have a SwissPass login? No problem, all you need is a valid e-mail address. You do not need a physical SwissPass card to get a SwissPass login.

Access your payment method easily without re-registering. 

You can pay for your parking tickets with TWINT, ApplePay, Mastercard, Visa, American Express, PostFinance Card, Diners Club and Reka. You can also use the payment methods saved in SBB Mobile to pay in the P+Rail app or the reverse (with the exception of monthly invoicing).

Save time with personalisation. 

Set up your favourite location in the P+Rail app and save several car registration numbers. That way you can get your parking ticket with even fewer clicks – it’s all possible with the app

Pilot services.

Reserve a parking space, view real-time occupancy or a Switzerland-wide overview of parking spaces?

Find a parking space easily. 

If you are travelling by car and can’t find a parking space, the P+Rail app shows you nearby P+Rail facilities.

  • Please note that you do not have a right to a parking space even if you buy a ticket in advance (no refunds). We therefore recommend that you park your car first and then buy a parking ticket wherever possible.
  • For detailed conditions, please see the current P+Rail GTCs.

Replacements for Internet Explorer 11.

Microsoft is gradually discontinuing its support for Internet Explorer 11 (more info on microsoft.comLink opens in new window.). If you continue to use Internet Explorer to access SBB.ch, you may experience functional limitations and display problems in the future. For this reason, we recommend using a more modern browser (e.g. Mozilla FirefoxLink opens in new window., Google ChromeLink opens in new window., Microsoft EdgeLink opens in new window.).

We are aware that switching to a new browser may take some getting used to and cause some uncertainties. A more modern browser will allow you to benefit from faster and more secure internet access in future. We are committed to continuing our work with the utmost enthusiasm in the future to guarantee you accessible and inclusive access to SBB.ch.
